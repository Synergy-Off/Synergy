About Synergy!

    Synergy is a mod for Half-Life 2.

    Modification is completely free and available in Steam!

    Synergy allows you to play together in the Half-Life series of games

    Available games in co-op mode:

  • Half Life 2

  • Half Life 2 Episode 1

  • Half Life 2 Episode 2

    For the correct operation of the game, you need to buy the above games in Steam:

  • Half Life 2

  • Half-Life 2 Episode 1 (Optional)

  • Half-Life 2 Episode 2 (Optional)
